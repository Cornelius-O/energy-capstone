# energy-capstone
Analyzing 55 years of global fuel prices and energy transition data across 314 countries using Python, SQL, and Tableau. Includes predictive forecasting with Facebook Prophet.
# Global Energy Transition & Fuel Price Analysis | 1970–2035

## Business Question
As fossil fuel prices grow more volatile, is the global shift to 
renewables accelerating fast enough — and when is the economic tipping point?

## Tools
Python · SQL · Pandas · Matplotlib · Tableau · Prophet

## Key Findings
- Renewable energy growth decoupled from oil price volatility post-2010
- Solar growth rate surpassed wind after 2020
- Prophet model forecasts renewables reaching ~20% of global energy by 2035
